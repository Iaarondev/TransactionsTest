Welcome to Negroar Resilience

Empowering and uplifting the Negro community in Baton Rouge through food service entrepreneurship.

About Us

Negroar Resilience is an initiative to empower individuals in Baton Rouge’s Negro community by providing access to professional kitchens and retail spaces. We aim to foster independence, resilience, and entrepreneurial success.
