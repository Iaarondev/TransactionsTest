About Negroar Resilience

Negroar Resilience is an initiative designed to empower individuals in the Negro community of Baton Rouge by providing access to a professional kitchen and retail space. Our mission is to foster independence, resilience, and success for entrepreneurs who lack the resources or certifications to start their food service businesses.

Our Program

Flexible Access

Access our professional kitchen and retail space with convenient weekly scheduling options.

Dedicated Time Slots

Guaranteed time slots for your business operations, allowing focused and uninterrupted work.

Prime Location

Centrally located facility in Baton Rouge, making it easily accessible to your customer base.

Our Commitment

We are committed to ensuring that Negroar Resilience complies with all safety and legal standards while staying true to its mission of empowerment. By joining forces with like-minded individuals and organizations, we aim to cultivate a legacy of resilience and achievement for our community.
